Install the Members App

📱

iPhone & iPad

  1. Open the portal in Safari and sign in with your Roots & Wings Google (Gmail) account.
  2. Tap the Share button — the square with an up-arrow ⬆️ at the bottom of the screen.
  3. In the menu that slides up, scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen.
  4. Tap Add in the top corner. The Roots & Wings icon appears on your home screen.
🤖

Android

  1. Open the portal in Chrome and sign in with your Roots & Wings Google (Gmail) account.
  2. Tap the ⋮ menu in the top-right corner.
  3. Tap Install app, then tap Install to confirm.
  4. The app installs to your apps list. See “Putting it on your home screen” below.
🏠 Putting it on your home screen (Android): After installing, the app lands in your app drawer — your full list of apps — and may not jump onto your home screen automatically. That's an Android setting, not a problem with the app. To add it: open your app drawer, press and hold the Roots & Wings icon, then drag it onto your home screen. (On iPhone, it goes straight to your home screen.)
⚠️ A note on browsers: Use Chrome on Android and Safari on iPhone. Avoid Brave on Android — it only creates a temporary shortcut that can disappear, instead of a real app.
