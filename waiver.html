Member Agreement & Waivers

A reference copy of the agreement families accept when registering with Roots and Wings Homeschool Co-op Inc.

Contents
  1. Membership Requirements & Expectations
  2. Photo & Media Consent
  3. Data Storage & AI Use
  4. Liability Waiver

1. Membership Requirements & Expectations

Requirements: Sign this agreement, pay the Fall Membership Fee, and ensure an assigned adult is present with your student(s) each co-op day.

Expectations: Attend co-op and parent meetings reasonably; participate in special events, field trips, and leadership/volunteering per the handbook; pay class fees and sign up for classes on time.

If these requirements or expectations don't work for your family, please contact board@rootsandwingsindy.com before registering.

2. Photo & Media Consent

I consent to Roots and Wings Homeschool Co-op photographing, filming, quoting, or interviewing my family for non-commercial co-op use — including the yearbook, social media, website, and other media — and release the co-op and its agents from any claims related to that use.

3. Data Storage & AI Use

To run the co-op we store family information — parent and student names, pronouns, home address, phone, email, birthdates, allergies and medical notes, emergency contacts, and any placement or accommodation notes — in Google Workspace under the rootsandwingsindy.com account. Access is limited to the Roots and Wings board and committee leads, and we don't share information outside of what's required to run the co-op (e.g., emergency services).

We also use AI tools, including Anthropic's Claude, to help draft communications and organize records. Family information may be processed by these tools under their enterprise privacy terms; AI is not used to make enrollment, placement, or disciplinary decisions without board review.

You can review, correct, or delete your family's stored information any time by emailing board@rootsandwingsindy.com.

4. Liability Waiver

I understand that my family's participation in Roots and Wings Homeschool Co-op Inc. activities — held primarily at First Mennonite Church — is voluntary and could result in injury, including serious injury or death. I affirm that my family is in good physical condition to participate.

In consideration of our participation, I release Roots and Wings Homeschool Co-op Inc., First Mennonite Church, and their agents and employees from any claims, demands, or causes of action arising from our participation, now or in the future.

Families accept this agreement by typing their legal name as an electronic signature during registration. Questions? Email board@rootsandwingsindy.com.
