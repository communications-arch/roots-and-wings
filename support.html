Ways to Support

Every little bit helps our community grow

Roots & Wings Indy is a non-profit run entirely by parent volunteers. Here are some easy ways you can support our co-op.

Kroger Community Rewards

Link your Kroger Card to Roots & Wings Indy Homeschool and every time you use your rewards card, donations are automatically given to our account. It costs you nothing extra — just shop like you normally do. Search for organization RA139.

Link Your Kroger Card

Box Tops for Education

Download the Box Tops app, purchase qualifying items, snap a photo of your receipt, and the earnings are automatically deposited into our account. It's that easy!

Download the App

Donate Directly via PayPal

Want to make a direct contribution? Every donation goes toward keeping our co-op running — from supplies and materials to facility costs. Any amount makes a difference!

Donate via PayPal
