Member Agreement & Waivers

By typing your name to sign below, you agree to all of the following on behalf of your family.

Membership Requirements & Expectations

Requirements: Sign this agreement, pay the Fall Membership Fee, and ensure an assigned adult is present with your student(s) each co-op day.

Expectations: Attend co-op and parent meetings reasonably; participate in special events, field trips, and leadership/volunteering per the handbook; pay class fees and sign up for classes on time.

Handbook: I acknowledge that I have received and read the Roots and Wings Homeschool Co-op Inc. Member Handbook and agree to work with co-op members to meet its requirements and expectations.

If these requirements or expectations don't work for your family, please contact board@rootsandwingsindy.com before registering.

Photo, Video & Quote Consent

I consent to Roots and Wings photographing, filming, and quoting me and the child(ren) named above, and using that content for co-op non-profit purposes — yearbook, website, newsletters, printed rosters, and the members portal. I release the co-op and its agents from any claims related to that use.

To opt out, check "Opt out of photo and film" next to me or any child above. We won't use that person's photo, video, or quote in any co-op material. Other adults in my household (co-parents, backup Learning Coaches) make this choice on their own waiver.

Data Storage & AI Use

To run the co-op we store family information — parent and student names, pronouns, home address, phone, email, birthdates, allergies and medical notes, emergency contacts, and any placement or accommodation notes — in Google Workspace. Access is limited to the Roots and Wings members, and we don't share information outside of what's required to run the co-op (e.g., emergency services).

We also use AI tools, including Anthropic's Claude, to help draft communications and organize records. Family information may be processed by these tools under their enterprise privacy terms; AI is not used to make enrollment, placement, or disciplinary decisions.

You can review, correct, or delete your family's stored information any time by emailing board@rootsandwingsindy.com.

I understand that my family's participation in Roots and Wings Homeschool Co-op Inc. activities — held primarily at First Mennonite Church — is voluntary and could result in injury, including serious injury or death. I affirm that my family is in good physical condition to participate.

In consideration of our participation, I release Roots and Wings Homeschool Co-op Inc., First Mennonite Church, and their agents and employees from any claims, demands, or causes of action arising from our participation, now or in the future.
