Member Requirements & Expectations

Member Requirements

  • Sign member agreement, photo release and liability waiver
  • Pay Fall Membership Fee upon registration
  • Active assigned adult will be present with student(s) each co-op day

Member Expectations

  • Reasonable family attendance at co-op
  • Reasonable participation at parent meetings
  • Reasonable participation in special events and field trips
  • Pay class fees on time before/on the first day of co-op each session
  • Sign up for classes on time
  • Active participation in the leadership and facilitation of daily co-op activities through volunteering, as outlined in the member handbook

If you feel you cannot meet the membership requirements and/or expectations, please contact the board at board@rootsandwingsindy.com.

Data Storage & AI Use

To run the co-op, we collect and store personally identifiable information about your family, including: parent and student names, pronouns, home address, phone number, email address, birthdates, allergies and medical notes, emergency contacts, and any placement or accommodation notes you share with us.

This information is stored in Google Workspace (Sheets, Drive, Gmail) under the rootsandwingsindy.com account. Access is limited to the Roots and Wings board and committee leads on a need-to-know basis. We do not sell or share your information with third parties outside of what's required to operate the co-op (e.g., emergency services).

We also use AI tools, including Anthropic's Claude, to help draft communications, organize records, and automate routine co-op tasks. When we use AI, family information may be processed by these tools under their standard enterprise privacy terms. We do not use AI to make enrollment, placement, or disciplinary decisions without board review.

You can request to review, correct, or delete your family's stored information at any time by emailing board@rootsandwingsindy.com.
