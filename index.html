About Us

Where Community Grows

Roots & Wings Homeschool, Inc. is a secular, inclusive, parent-led homeschool cooperative serving families with preschool through high school students from the greater Indianapolis area. For more than 20 years, we've brought families together through hands-on classes, shared activities, and meaningful connections — all in a welcoming environment built on mutual respect. Today about 40 families call Roots & Wings home.

This is a true co-op — every parent leads or assists throughout the entire co-op day. It's not a drop-off program; it's a community we build together. Our facility includes classrooms, a kitchen, a large gathering space, playground, pavilion, and picnic areas that we use extensively — especially for our younger kids. You'll often find classes happening outside around a picnic table or under the pavilion, weather permitting.

Our Commitment to Inclusivity

We are committed to creating an environment in which everyone's dignity is respected. We will not tolerate discrimination on the basis of race, color, sex, sexual orientation, gender identity, class, religious affiliation or non-affiliation, age, parental or marital status, veteran status, national or ethnic origin, legal status, political beliefs, or physical or mental status. We are dedicated to fostering an empathetic, compassionate, and socially responsible community.

What We Offer

A Day at Roots & Wings

At Roots & Wings, it's all about connection. We're a secular, inclusive co-op that welcomes all families — no matter your background, beliefs, or learning style. We gather every Wednesday from August through May for classes that spark curiosity, build friendships, and let kids learn alongside their peers. Our year is divided into five-week sessions with short breaks in between and a longer rest over the winter holidays.

Wednesday Routine

9:40 – 9:45 am Arrive
9:55 am Announcements in Fellowship Hall
10:00 am – 12:00 pm Morning Classes grouped by age
12:00 – 1:00 pm Lunch & Parent-Supervised Outdoor Play
1:00 – 1:55 pm 1st Afternoon Elective mixed ages
2:00 – 2:55 pm 2nd Afternoon Elective mixed ages
2:55 – 3:15 pm Clean Up

Delays and cancellations due to weather or calendar changes are posted to chat announcements.

Our Age Groups

Morning classes are grouped by age with a cap of about 12 students per class. Afternoon electives mix things up so kids can learn alongside different peers.

Note: Families with children both inside and outside this range may attend, but programming is only available for students within the co-op's age range.

Beyond Wednesdays

Field Trips

Frequent optional outings during the week — museums, farms, nature centers, and more around Indianapolis.

Parents Nights

Time for the grown-ups to connect, plan, and recharge together.

Spring Camp

A 3-day outdoor adventure camp that's a highlight of the year for kids of all ages.

Passion Fair

Like a science fair, but for any subject! Kids get to display and present their current favorite topic — dinosaurs, crochet, robotics, ancient Egypt, you name it.

Maker's Market

Members of all ages — kids and adults — set up shop to sell their handmade wares. Jewelry, baked goods, art, woodwork, and more.

Field Day

Our end-of-year celebration with games, activities, and a whole lot of fun to close out the season.

And that's not all — we also host a school dance, ice cream social, and more! Our families are always dreaming up new ways to connect.
