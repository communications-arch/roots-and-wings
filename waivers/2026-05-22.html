Member Agreement & Waivers

Archived snapshot of version 2026-05-22 — the agreement that took effect on May 22, 2026.

Archived snapshot (v2026-05-22). This page is shown so members and staff can see the exact text someone agreed to when they signed under this version. The current waiver is at waiver.html — it may have been updated since.

1. Membership Requirements & Expectations

Requirements: Sign this agreement, pay the Fall Membership Fee, and ensure an assigned adult is present with your student(s) each co-op day.

Expectations: Attend co-op and parent meetings reasonably; participate in special events, field trips, and leadership/volunteering per the handbook; pay class fees and sign up for classes on time.

Handbook: I acknowledge that I have received and read the Roots and Wings Homeschool Co-op Inc. Member Handbook and agree to work with co-op members to meet its requirements and expectations.

If these requirements or expectations don't work for your family, please contact board@rootsandwingsindy.com before registering.

2. Photo, Video & Quote Consent

I consent to Roots and Wings photographing, filming, and quoting me, and using that content for co-op non-profit purposes — yearbook, website, newsletters, printed rosters, and the members portal. I release the co-op and its agents from any claims related to that use.

To opt out, check "Opt out of photo and film" below. We won't use my photo, video, or quote in any co-op material.

3. Data Storage & AI Use

Personal data privacy is important to Roots & Wings, and we are committed to member data privacy. We collect names of learning coaches and children, pronouns (optional), home address (optional), phone, email, birthdates, allergies and medical notes (optional), and any placement or accommodation notes (optional) for organizational use.

This data is shared and stored in Google Workspace and a secure database. Access is limited to the Roots and Wings members. We use AI tools, including Anthropic's Claude, to assist with the technical development of our member portal. When used for development purposes, only the structural format of our data systems is shared with AI tools. No personal data is shared.

Roots & Wings does not currently share personal data with AI (including LLMs) or outside entities other than Google Workspace. If in the future that may be considered, the proposal will be presented to Membership for approval.

4. Liability Waiver

I understand that my family's participation in Roots and Wings Homeschool Co-op Inc. activities — held primarily at First Mennonite Church — is voluntary and could result in injury, including serious injury or death. I affirm that my family is in good physical condition to participate.

In consideration of our participation, I release Roots and Wings Homeschool Co-op Inc., First Mennonite Church, and their agents and employees from any claims, demands, or causes of action arising from our participation, now or in the future.
